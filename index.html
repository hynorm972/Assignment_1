Paneer

Paneer is a fresh cheese used in a wide variety of Indian recipes, made by heating and then curdling milk using acid. It's very mild and milky in flavor, white in color, and its texture is soft, spongy, and squeaky. This texture helps it to absorb the flavors of sauces or marinades.

Mushroom

Mushrooms are fungi. They belong in a kingdom of their own, separate from plants and animals. Fungi differ from plants and animals in the way they obtain their nutrients. Generally, plants make their food using the sun's energy (photosynthesis), while animals eat, then internally digest, their food.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish that features medium-grained rice cooked in vinegar, served with raw or cooked seafood and a variety of toppings or fillings.It is generally has a strong flavor but is largely neutral, tangy, and sweet. It is high in Vitamin B12, which keeps the brain 'happy'
